About Gamma

Who remembers their first video game? Who remembers that one breathtaking story? How about that one character that made you dream of something bigger than life. Gamma Interactive is a mobile gaming company founded by two such dreamers who played their first video game before they can even walk. Welcome to our imagination.

Game Development

With over 40 years of combined work experience in the entertainment and marketing fields, our development team designs and produces products through rigid, standardized creative and business methodologies. Though we currently have a focus on mobile titles, we have the know-how and resources to scale to fit the demands of our clients. We are currently looking to grow with the ability to take on multiple concurrent projects. Let us know when we can get started on your project today!
